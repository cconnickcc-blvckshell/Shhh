## 1. Requirement

Shhh is an adults-only platform. You must be at least 18 years of age (or the age of majority in your jurisdiction) to use our Services.

---

## 2. How We Verify

### 2.1 Self-Declaration

When you create an account, you confirm that you meet the age requirement. This is the minimum verification we require.

### 2.2 Photo Verification

For certain features (e.g. higher verification tier), we may require photo verification. You submit a selfie that we compare to your profile photos. This helps ensure the account holder is the person in the profile.

### 2.3 ID Verification

For the highest verification tier, we may require submission of an ID document hash or verification through a third-party provider. We do not store your full ID document.

---

## 3. Failure to Verify

If you cannot or do not complete verification when required, access to certain features may be restricted.

---

## 4. Fraudulent Verification

Submitting false information or fraudulent documents may result in immediate account termination and reporting to authorities.
