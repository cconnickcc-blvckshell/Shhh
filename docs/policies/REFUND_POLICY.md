## 2. Subscription Refunds

2.1 **During trial:** If you cancel during a free trial, you will not be charged.

2.2 **After purchase:** Subscription fees are generally non-refundable. We may consider refunds in the following circumstances:

- **Technical failure:** The service was unavailable or materially defective through our fault
- **Billing error:** You were charged in error (e.g. duplicate charge, wrong amount)
- **Statutory right to withdraw (EU/UK consumers):** If you are a consumer in the EU, EEA, or UK, you have the right to withdraw from a distance contract within 14 days of purchase without giving a reason. You may request a full refund within this period. **Exception:** If you have expressly requested that we begin performance during the withdrawal period (e.g. you have started using premium features), your right to withdraw may be lost once performance has begun with your consent. We will inform you before you confirm purchase.
- **Other cooling-off periods:** Consumers in other jurisdictions (e.g. Australia) may have similar statutory rights; we will honour them where applicable.

2.3 **Pro-rata refunds:** We do not offer pro-rata refunds for partial subscription periods. Cancellation stops future renewals but does not refund the current period.

2.4 **Refund method and timing:** Refunds are issued to the original payment method. We aim to process refunds within 5–10 business days of approval. Depending on your bank or card issuer, it may take additional time for the refund to appear on your statement.
