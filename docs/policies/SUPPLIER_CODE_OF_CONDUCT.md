# Supplier Code of Conduct

**Effective Date:** [EFFECTIVE_DATE]  
**Audience:** Suppliers of [COMPANY_NAME]  
**Owner:** Procurement

---

## 1. Introduction

[COMPANY_NAME] expects its suppliers to operate ethically and in compliance with applicable laws. This Code of Conduct sets minimum standards for labour, human rights, and business conduct.

---

## 2. Labour and Human Rights

### 2.1 Forced Labour

- No forced, bonded, or compulsory labour
- No retention of identity documents or wages
- Workers must be free to leave employment with reasonable notice

### 2.2 Child Labour

- No child labour. Minimum age for employment must comply with applicable law (and not less than 15, or 14 where permitted by ILO Convention 138)

### 2.3 Human Trafficking

- No human trafficking or modern slavery
- No recruitment or employment practices that exploit workers

### 2.4 Freedom of Association

- Respect workers' rights to join or form unions and bargain collectively, where permitted by law

### 2.5 Non-Discrimination

- No discrimination based on race, colour, religion, gender, sexual orientation, nationality, disability, or other protected characteristics

### 2.6 Harassment and Abuse

- No harassment, abuse, or inhumane treatment of workers

### 2.7 Wages and Hours

- Comply with applicable wage and hour laws
- Provide working hours that comply with law and industry standards

---

## 3. Health and Safety

- Provide a safe and healthy work environment
- Comply with applicable health and safety regulations

---

## 4. Environment

- Comply with applicable environmental laws
- Minimise environmental impact where practicable

---

## 5. Ethics and Integrity

- No bribery or corruption
- Accurate and transparent business records
- Protect confidential information

---

## 6. Compliance

Suppliers are expected to:

- Implement policies and procedures to meet these standards
- Conduct due diligence on their own supply chains
- Cooperate with us in assessments and audits
- Remediate promptly if violations are found

---

## 7. Non-Compliance

Failure to meet these standards may result in termination of the business relationship.
