## 1. Our Community

Shhh is a privacy-native, proximity-driven platform for adults. We aim to foster a respectful, consensual, and safe environment. These guidelines help you understand what we expect and what we support.

---

## 2. Core Principles

### 2.1 Consent

- Respect others' boundaries. Consent is ongoing and can be withdrawn at any time.
- Do not pressure others into sharing, meeting, or doing anything they are not comfortable with.
- Do not share intimate content without explicit consent.

### 2.2 Respect

- Treat others with dignity and respect.
- Disagreements are normal; harassment and abuse are not.
- We welcome diverse identities, orientations, and preferences. Hate speech and discrimination are not tolerated.

### 2.3 Honesty

- Be authentic. Use accurate photos and information.
- Do not impersonate others or misrepresent yourself.

### 2.4 Safety

- Use the in-app safety features (report, block, emergency contacts) when needed.
- Report concerning behavior. We take reports seriously.

---

## 3. What We Support

- Genuine connections and respectful conversations;
- Expressing preferences and boundaries clearly;
- Privacy and control over your profile and data;
- Diverse communities and identities;
- Fun, flirty, and adult-oriented content within our rules.

---

## 4. What We Don't Allow

- **Harassment:** Repeated unwanted contact, threats, or intimidation;
- **Non-consensual content:** Sharing intimate imagery without consent;
- **Minors:** Any content or conduct involving minors;
- **Exploitation:** Human trafficking, coercion, or grooming;
- **Violence:** Threats or promotion of violence;
- **Hate:** Hate speech or discrimination;
- **Spam:** Unsolicited commercial messages or bulk outreach;
- **Impersonation:** Pretending to be someone else.

---

## 5. Content and Profiles

- **Photos:** Use your own photos. Do not use others' images without permission.
- **Profile text:** Be accurate. Avoid misleading or deceptive descriptions.
- **Messages:** Respect consent. Do not send unsolicited explicit content.

---

## 6. Reporting and Enforcement

- Use the in-app report feature for violations.
- We review reports and take action in accordance with our [Content Moderation Policy](CONTENT_MODERATION_POLICY.md).
- You may appeal decisions through our [Appeal Process](APPEAL_PROCESS.md).
