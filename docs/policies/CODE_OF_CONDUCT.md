# Code of Conduct

**Effective Date:** [EFFECTIVE_DATE]  
**Classification:** Internal  
**Audience:** Employees, Contractors, Volunteers  
**Owner:** HR / Leadership

---

## 1. Purpose

[COMPANY_NAME] is committed to a workplace that is respectful, inclusive, and free from harassment and discrimination. This Code of Conduct sets expectations for all team members.

---

## 2. Our Values

- **Integrity:** Act honestly and ethically
- **Respect:** Treat everyone with dignity
- **Accountability:** Take responsibility for your actions
- **Inclusion:** Foster a diverse and welcoming environment

---

## 3. Expected Behavior

- Treat colleagues, users, and partners with respect
- Communicate professionally and constructively
- Protect confidential and personal data
- Comply with all applicable laws and company policies
- Report concerns through appropriate channels

---

## 4. Prohibited Behavior

- **Harassment:** Unwelcome conduct based on race, gender, religion, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics
- **Discrimination:** Treating people unfairly based on protected characteristics
- **Retaliation:** Punishing anyone for reporting concerns in good faith
- **Conflict of interest:** Undisclosed conflicts that could affect judgment
- **Fraud or misconduct:** Dishonesty, theft, or violation of law or policy

---

## 5. Reporting

If you experience or witness a violation, report to:
- Your manager
- HR: [HR_EMAIL]
- Whistleblower channel: See [Whistleblower Policy](WHISTLEBLOWER_POLICY.md)

Reports will be investigated promptly and confidentially. Retaliation against good-faith reporters is prohibited.

---

## 6. Consequences

Violations may result in corrective action, up to and including termination of employment or contract.

---

## 7. Acknowledgment

All team members are expected to read and acknowledge this Code of Conduct. Questions? Contact [HR_EMAIL].
