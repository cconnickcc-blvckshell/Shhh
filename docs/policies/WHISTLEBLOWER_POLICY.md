# Whistleblower Policy

**Effective Date:** [EFFECTIVE_DATE]  
**Classification:** Internal  
**Audience:** Employees, Contractors  
**Owner:** Legal / HR

---

## 1. Purpose

[COMPANY_NAME] encourages the reporting of concerns about wrongdoing, including fraud, misconduct, legal violations, or ethical breaches. This policy protects reporters and outlines the process.

---

## 2. Scope

You may report concerns about:

- Fraud or financial misconduct
- Violations of law or regulation
- Breaches of company policy
- Harassment, discrimination, or retaliation
- Safety or environmental issues
- Human trafficking or modern slavery (see [Human Trafficking Policy](HUMAN_TRAFFICKING_POLICY.md))

---

## 3. How to Report

- **Internal:** [WHISTLEBLOWER_EMAIL] or [WHISTLEBLOWER_PHONE]
- **Anonymous:** Anonymous reporting may be available via a third-party service
- **Manager or HR:** You may also report to your manager or HR, who will escalate as appropriate

---

## 4. Protection Against Retaliation

- We prohibit retaliation against anyone who reports concerns in good faith
- Retaliation includes dismissal, demotion, harassment, or any adverse action
- If you believe you have been retaliated against, report it immediately

---

## 5. Process

- Reports are received and triaged by designated personnel
- Investigations are conducted confidentially
- You may be contacted for additional information
- Outcomes are communicated where appropriate (subject to privacy and legal constraints)

---

## 6. Confidentiality

We will protect your identity to the extent possible. In some cases, disclosure may be required by law or necessary for a fair investigation.

---

## 7. Good Faith

Reports should be made in good faith. Knowingly false reports may result in disciplinary action.
