**Effective Date:** [EFFECTIVE_DATE]

**Owner:** Trust & Safety

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy describes how [COMPANY_NAME] moderates content and conduct on the Shhh platform to maintain a safe and respectful community.

---

## 2. Moderation Scope & Capacity

**Moderation is best-effort.** We use a combination of human and automated moderation. We do not guarantee:

- 24/7 availability
- Specific response times or SLAs
- That every report will be reviewed within a certain timeframe

We aim to review reports promptly and take action where appropriate. We do not disclose internal moderation capacity or staffing. If you are in immediate danger, contact law enforcement or emergency services.

---

## 3. Moderation Approach

- **Proactive:** Where possible, automated and manual review of reported content and high-risk areas
- **Reactive:** Investigation of user reports
- **Proportional:** Actions match the severity of the violation

---

## 4. Content Review

### 4.1 What We Review

- Profile photos and bios
- Messages and whispers (when reported or flagged)
- Venue and event descriptions
- User reports and appeals

### 4.2 Review Criteria

Content is assessed against our [Acceptable Use Policy](ACCEPTABLE_USE_POLICY.md) and [Community Guidelines](COMMUNITY_GUIDELINES.md). We consider context, intent, and impact.

---

## 5. Actions

| Action | When |
|--------|------|
| **Warning** | First-time minor violation |
| **Content removal** | Content violates policies |
| **Temporary suspension** | Repeated or moderate violation |
| **Permanent ban** | Severe or repeated violation; illegal content |
| **Report to authorities** | Child safety; illegal activity; credible threats |

---

## 6. Appeals

Users may appeal moderation decisions through our [Appeal Process](APPEAL_PROCESS.md). Appeals are reviewed by a different moderator or senior team member where available.

---

## 7. Transparency

We do not disclose specific moderation decisions to third parties except as required by law. We may publish aggregate moderation statistics in our transparency report.

---

## 8. Moderator Standards

Moderators are trained on our policies and must act fairly and consistently. Decisions are logged for audit.
