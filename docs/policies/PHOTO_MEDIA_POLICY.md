# Photo/Media Policy

**Effective Date:** [EFFECTIVE_DATE]  
**Last Updated:** [EFFECTIVE_DATE]

---

## 1. Overview

This policy covers photos and other media you upload to Shhh, including profile photos, album content, and shared media.

---

## 2. What You Can Upload

- **Profile photos:** Photos of yourself for your profile
- **Album media:** Photos and videos in private or shared albums
- **Chat attachments:** Media sent in messages (subject to conversation settings)

---

## 3. Rules

- **Ownership:** Only upload content you own or have permission to use
- **Consent:** Do not upload intimate content of others without their consent
- **No minors:** Never upload content depicting minors
- **No illegal content:** Content must comply with our [Acceptable Use Policy](ACCEPTABLE_USE_POLICY.md)

---

## 4. Storage and Retention

- Media is stored on our infrastructure
- **Self-destructing media:** Media with TTL is automatically deleted after the set period
- **Account deletion:** Your media is deleted when your account is deleted

---

## 5. Sharing and Privacy

- **Albums:** You control who can view shared albums. Revoking share access removes access for that user.
- **Profile photos:** Visibility depends on your profile and discovery settings
- **Blur/reveal:** Some features allow you to control when others can see unblurred photos

---

## 6. Removal

We may remove media that violates our policies. You may delete your own media at any time through the app.
