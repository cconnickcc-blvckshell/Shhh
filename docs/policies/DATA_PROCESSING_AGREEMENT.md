# Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

**Effective Date:** [EFFECTIVE_DATE]  
**Parties:** [COMPANY_NAME] (Data Controller) and [CUSTOMER_NAME] (Data Processor)  
**Purpose:** GDPR Art. 28 compliant agreement for processing of personal data

---

## 1. Definitions

- **Controller:** [COMPANY_NAME]
- **Processor:** [CUSTOMER_NAME]
- **Personal Data:** As defined in GDPR Art. 4(1)
- **Processing:** As defined in GDPR Art. 4(2)
- **Sub-processor:** Any processor engaged by the Processor

---

## 2. Scope

This DPA applies when the Processor processes Personal Data on behalf of the Controller in connection with the services described in the main agreement between the parties.

---

## 3. Processing Instructions

3.1 The Processor shall process Personal Data only on documented instructions from the Controller, including with regard to transfers.

3.2 The Processor shall not process Personal Data for any purpose other than as instructed.

3.3 If the Processor is required by law to process Personal Data, it shall inform the Controller before processing unless prohibited.

---

## 4. Confidentiality and Security

4.1 The Processor shall ensure that persons authorized to process Personal Data are bound by confidentiality obligations.

4.2 The Processor shall implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk, including:

- Encryption of data in transit and at rest;
- Access controls and authentication;
- Regular testing and evaluation of security measures.

---

## 5. Sub-processors

5.1 The Processor shall not engage a Sub-processor without prior written authorization from the Controller.

5.2 Where a Sub-processor is engaged, the same data protection obligations as set out in this DPA shall be imposed on the Sub-processor by contract.

5.3 The Processor remains liable to the Controller for the performance of the Sub-processor's obligations.

---

## 6. Data Subject Rights

6.1 The Processor shall assist the Controller in responding to requests from data subjects exercising their rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability, etc.).

6.2 The Processor shall not respond to such requests without the Controller's instruction, unless required by law.

---

## 7. Breach Notification

The Processor shall notify the Controller without undue delay upon becoming aware of a personal data breach, and shall provide sufficient information to allow the Controller to meet its breach notification obligations.

---

## 8. Audits

The Processor shall make available to the Controller all information necessary to demonstrate compliance with this DPA and shall allow for and contribute to audits and inspections.

---

## 9. Deletion or Return

Upon termination of the services, the Processor shall delete or return all Personal Data at the Controller's choice, unless required to retain by law.

---

## 10. Liability

Liability under this DPA shall be subject to the limitations in the main agreement, except that the Processor shall be liable for the acts or omissions of its Sub-processors.

---

## 11. Governing Law

This DPA shall be governed by the laws of [JURISDICTION]. For EU data subjects, the Standard Contractual Clauses (where applicable) shall apply.
