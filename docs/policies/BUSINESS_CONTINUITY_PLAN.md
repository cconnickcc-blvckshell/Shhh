## 3. Disruption Scenarios

| Scenario | Response |
|----------|----------|
| **Data centre outage** | Failover to backup region; restore from backups |
| **Cyber incident** | Follow [Security Incident Response Plan](SECURITY_INCIDENT_RESPONSE_PLAN.md) |
| **Key personnel unavailable** | Cross-training; delegation of authority |
| **Pandemic / remote work** | Remote work protocols; ensure access to systems |
