## 1. Types of Requests

We accept requests to remove content in the following circumstances:

- **Copyright (DMCA):** See our [DMCA Policy](DMCA_POLICY.md)
- **Trademark:** Unauthorized use of your trademark
- **Defamation:** False statements that harm your reputation
- **Privacy:** Your personal information posted without consent
- **Non-consensual intimate imagery:** Intimate images/videos shared without your consent
- **Other legal:** Court order, regulatory requirement

---

## 2. How to Submit

**Email:** [LEGAL_EMAIL] or [CONTENT_REMOVAL_EMAIL]

Include:

- Your name and contact information
- Type of request (copyright, trademark, privacy, etc.)
- Identification of the content (URL, user ID, screenshot)
- Explanation and supporting documentation
- For legal requests: copy of court order or legal basis

---

## 3. Process

- We will acknowledge receipt and review your request
- We may request additional information
- We will take action as required by law and our policies
- We will notify you of the outcome where appropriate

---

## 4. Appeals

If you are the uploader and your content was removed, you may appeal via our [Appeal Process](APPEAL_PROCESS.md) or, for DMCA, via counter-notification (see [DMCA Policy](DMCA_POLICY.md)).
