## 5. Risk Assessment

We have assessed our operations and supply chains for modern slavery risks:

- **Operations:** Our workforce is primarily employed in [jurisdictions]. We use direct employment and reputable contractors.
- **Supply chain:** Our key suppliers include cloud providers, payment processors, and technology vendors. We prioritise suppliers with strong ethical and labour practices.
- **Platform:** We have policies and systems to prevent misuse of our platform for trafficking or exploitation (see our [Acceptable Use Policy](ACCEPTABLE_USE_POLICY.md) and [Community Guidelines](COMMUNITY_GUIDELINES.md)).

## 6. Due Diligence

We conduct due diligence on new and existing suppliers:

- Ongoing monitoring and assessment

## 9. Remediation

If we identify modern slavery or human trafficking in our operations or supply chain:

- We will take immediate steps to address the situation and support affected individuals where possible
- We will work with suppliers to implement corrective action plans
- We may suspend or terminate relationships where remediation is not possible or the supplier does not cooperate
- We will report to authorities where required by law
