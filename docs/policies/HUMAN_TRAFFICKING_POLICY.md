# Human Trafficking Policy

**Effective Date:** [EFFECTIVE_DATE]  
**Classification:** Internal  
**Owner:** Legal / Compliance  
**Audience:** All Employees, Contractors

---

## 1. Purpose

[COMPANY_NAME] has zero tolerance for human trafficking and modern slavery. This policy establishes our commitment and procedures to prevent, detect, and respond to trafficking in our operations, supply chain, and platform.

---

## 2. Definitions

- **Human trafficking:** The recruitment, transportation, harbouring, or receipt of persons for the purpose of exploitation (e.g. forced labour, sexual exploitation).
- **Modern slavery:** Encompasses trafficking, forced labour, debt bondage, and similar practices.

---

## 3. Our Commitment

We will not:

- tolerate or facilitate human trafficking or modern slavery;
- use forced or compulsory labour;
- use child labour;
- engage in or support practices that exploit or degrade workers.

---

## 4. Platform Safety

We take steps to prevent misuse of our platform for trafficking or exploitation:

- **Acceptable Use Policy:** Prohibits exploitation, coercion, and related conduct. See [Acceptable Use Policy](ACCEPTABLE_USE_POLICY.md).
- **Moderation:** We review reported content and take action against violations.
- **Reporting:** Users can report concerning behaviour. We cooperate with law enforcement when required.

---

## 5. Supply Chain

We expect suppliers to comply with our [Supplier Code of Conduct](SUPPLIER_CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) and conduct due diligence as described in our [Supply Chain Due Diligence](SUPPLY_CHAIN_DUE_DILIGENCE.md) procedure.

---

## 6. Reporting

- **Internal:** Report concerns to [HR_EMAIL] or via our [Whistleblower Policy](WHISTLEBLOWER_POLICY.md).
- **External:** Report to law enforcement or relevant authorities (e.g. National Human Trafficking Hotline).

---

## 7. Response

If we become aware of trafficking or modern slavery:

- We will investigate promptly;
- We will take appropriate action (e.g. suspension, termination, reporting to authorities);
- We will support affected individuals where possible;
- We will document and learn from incidents.

---

## 8. Training

Relevant staff receive training on trafficking and modern slavery, including how to identify and report concerns.

---

## 9. Review

This policy is reviewed annually and updated as needed.
