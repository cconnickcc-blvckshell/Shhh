# Appeal Process

**Effective Date:** [EFFECTIVE_DATE]  
**Last Updated:** [EFFECTIVE_DATE]

---

## 1. Purpose

If your account has been restricted, suspended, or banned, or if your content was removed, you may appeal the decision. This document explains how.

---

## 2. How to Appeal

- **In-app:** If available, use the appeal option in the notification you received
- **Email:** [APPEALS_EMAIL] with:
  - Your account identifier (phone or user ID)
  - The decision you are appealing (e.g. ban, content removal)
  - Your explanation and any supporting information

---

## 3. What to Expect

3.1 **Acknowledgment:** We will acknowledge your appeal within 5 business days.

3.2 **Review:** A different moderator or senior team member will review your case. We aim to complete reviews within 14 business days.

3.3 **Outcome:** We will notify you of the outcome. Possible outcomes:
- **Upheld:** Original decision stands
- **Reversed:** Restriction lifted; content restored if applicable
- **Modified:** Different action (e.g. reduced suspension period)

---

## 4. One Appeal Per Decision

We generally allow one appeal per moderation decision. Repeated appeals for the same decision may not be reviewed.

---

## 5. Abuse of Appeals

Frivolous or abusive appeals (e.g. harassment of moderators) may result in additional restrictions.

---

## 6. External Options

If you believe we have not handled your case fairly, you may have the right to complain to a relevant authority (e.g. data protection authority for privacy-related matters).
