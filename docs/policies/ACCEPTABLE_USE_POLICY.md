## 2. Prohibited Conduct

### 2.1 Illegal Activity

You may not use the Services to:

- Violate any applicable law, regulation, or legal obligation;
- Engage in fraud, money laundering, or terrorism financing;
- Distribute illegal drugs or controlled substances;
- Facilitate human trafficking or exploitation;
- Engage in child sexual exploitation or abuse.

### 2.2 Harassment and Abuse

You may not:

- Harass, bully, threaten, or intimidate others;
- Stalk or repeatedly contact users who have blocked you or asked you to stop;
- Send unwanted sexual content or solicitations;
- Engage in hate speech or discrimination based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics;
- Doxx or publish another person's private contact information, address, or location with intent to harass or harm;
- Engage in romance scams, dating fraud, or financial exploitation (e.g. soliciting money under false pretences);
- Blackmail, extort, or threaten to expose private information.

### 2.3 Impersonation and Misrepresentation

You may not:

- Impersonate another person, entity, or brand;
- Create fake or misleading profiles;
- Use the Services to deceive or defraud others.

### 2.4 Spam and Abuse of Systems

You may not:

- Send spam, unsolicited messages, or bulk communications;
- Use automated tools (bots, scrapers) to access the Services without permission;
- Circumvent rate limits, security measures, or access controls;
- Attempt to gain unauthorized access to our systems, other users' accounts, or third-party data;
- Create fake or inauthentic accounts, artificially inflate engagement, or otherwise manipulate the platform.

### 2.5 Harmful Content

You may not:

- Share content that promotes violence, self-harm, or suicide;
- Share content that exploits or endangers minors;
- Share content that depicts non-consensual intimate imagery;
- Share malware, viruses, or other harmful code.

### 2.6 Commercial Misuse

You may not:

- Use the Services for unauthorized commercial purposes (e.g. spam, advertising, or solicitation without our consent);
- Resell or redistribute access to the Services;
- Use the Services to promote competing products or services without our permission.
