## 1. Overview

Under GDPR, CCPA, and similar laws, users have rights regarding their personal data. This document describes how [COMPANY_NAME] handles these requests.

---

## 2. Rights Summary

| Right | Description | Response Time |
|-------|-------------|---------------|
| **Access** | Copy of personal data we hold | 30 days (GDPR); 45 days (CCPA, extendable once by 45 days with notice) |
| **Rectification** | Correction of inaccurate data | 30 days |
| **Erasure** | Deletion of personal data | 30 days |
| **Portability** | Data in machine-readable format | 30 days |
| **Object** | Object to processing based on legitimate interests | 30 days |
| **Restrict** | Restrict processing in certain cases | 30 days |
| **Withdraw consent** | Where processing is consent-based | Immediate |

**Fees:** Access requests are free. Under GDPR, we may charge a reasonable fee or refuse manifestly unfounded or excessive requests (including repetitive requests). We will explain any fee or refusal.

## 4. Verification

We will verify identity before fulfilling requests. We may request additional information (e.g. phone number, account details, government-issued ID in limited cases) to confirm the requester is the data subject. We will not request more information than is reasonably necessary. We may refuse requests that are manifestly unfounded or excessive, including repetitive requests.

---

## 5. Process

5.1 **Acknowledgment:** We acknowledge receipt within 72 hours.

5.2 **Verification:** We verify identity and scope of the request.

5.3 **Fulfillment:** We fulfill the request within the statutory timeframe. Complex or manifestly unfounded requests may be extended (GDPR: up to 60 days) with notice.

5.4 **Refusal:** If we refuse a request, we explain the reason and inform the user of their right to complain to a supervisory authority.

---

## 6. Authorised Agents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you may designate an authorised agent to submit requests on your behalf. We will verify the agent's authority (e.g. signed permission, power of attorney) and may require you to verify your identity directly. Authorised agent requests may be submitted to [PRIVACY_EMAIL].

---

## 7. Appeals (CCPA)

If we deny your request (in whole or in part), you may appeal by contacting [PRIVACY_EMAIL] within 15 days of our response. We will conduct an internal review and respond within 45 days. If we uphold the denial, we will inform you of your right to lodge a complaint with the California Attorney General or pursue other options.
